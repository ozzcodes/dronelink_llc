About us

OzzyCodes Drone Services, LLC an emerging company, working with Aquiline Drones Drone on Demand program, to provide services for all customers in need. From aerial photography for many different occasions, to aerial mapping, and industrial inspections. We ensure safety safe working practices and customer satisfactions for each and every project. 
 
Our range of operations include mapping, surveying, inspection, data collection and marketing. OCD, LLC is fully insured and FAA Part 107 certified.

 

Our core services include: 

  • Drone Photography
  • Drone Video Production
  • Industrial Inspections utilizing Drones
  • Aerial Imaging Surveys
  • Panoramic Aerial Images
  • Aerial Virtual Tours
  • Video Production
  • Commercial Photography
  • Real Estate Photography
  • Event Photography and Video
  • Virtual Tours for Real Estate
  • High Resolution Panoramic Photography
  • Corporate Photography & Headshots
  • Website Design for Business & Real Estate

OzzyCodes Drone Services, LLC provides a complete air to ground service across New England, with the integration with Aquiline Drone's dedicated cloud infrastructure.
